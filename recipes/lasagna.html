Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

You should eat some. Thank me later.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) marinara sauce
  • 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Brown 1 pound of ground beef in a skillet.
  2. Add 1 jar (24 ounces) of marinara sauce and simmer.
  3. Combine 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese, 1 egg, and 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  4. Boil 12 lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Layer meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese in a 9x13 inch baking dish, repeating layers and ending with mozzarella on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  8. Let cool for 15 minutes before serving.
