Moroccan Chicken Tagine

Description

Moroccan Chicken Tagine is a savory and aromatic dish featuring tender chicken slow-cooked with potatoes, olives, and a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, and saffron, creating a rich, flavorful stew. The dish is traditionally served with couscous or bread, offering a comforting and authentic taste of Moroccan cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (thighs or drumsticks)
  • Potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • Green and/or black olives
  • Onions, sliced
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Garlic, minced
  • Fresh cilantro and parsley, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Chicken broth or water
  • Lemon (for zest and juice)
  • Spices: cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, ginger, saffron, salt, and pepper

Steps

  1. Rub chicken pieces with cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, ginger, saffron, salt, and pepper.
  2. Brown the chicken in olive oil, then set aside.
  3. Saut√© sliced onions and minced garlic in the same pot until soft.
  4. Add potato chunks, browned chicken, and enough chicken broth or water to partially submerge the chicken.
  5. Add chopped cilantro and parsley. Simmer covered for 45-60 minutes until chicken and potatoes are tender.
  6. Add olives, lemon zest, and juice. Cook for 10 more minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and parsley. Serve hot with couscous or bread.
